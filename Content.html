Consistency is Key

It is important that you have consistent tags for your content. Make sure you close all tags. If not, the content on your page may not display properly. It is better to have a <p > or <h> tag for each text item as it will help when you have to style your fonts in the next section.

In addition, your images should be properly sourced. If it is a local file, be sure to include it in your folder in the cloud 9 workspace. Otherwise, the image may not display properly.

All links and file links are case sensitive. Make sure to check the text. Otherwise the file will not display properly.
